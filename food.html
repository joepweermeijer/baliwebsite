Nasi Goreng

Nasi Goreng consists of fried rice with vegetables and sometimes chicken. The chicken is optional and not necessary. Nasi goreng comes in various forms. You can choose whether you want your fried rice with chicken or shrimp. You can also opt for a mie goreng. There the Nasi goreng is prepared with noodles instead of rice. Often the nasi or mie goreng is served with prawn crackers, a fried egg and matching vegetables.

Beef rendang

Just like nasi goreng, you can get this dish at the local warungs on the street. Beef rendang is made from beef. The meat is served with vegetables and deliciously seasoned. The meat is cooked in coconut milk and spices. Rice is also served with the meat and vegetables. Not only does rendang consist of beef, but there are also variants with chicken, goat or duck.

Chicken satay

You should not skip sate on your trip through Bali. The satay is one of the tastiest dishes you can eat on the island. Satay is marinated, skewered and grilled meat, served with spicy sauce, and consists of diced or sliced ​​chicken, goat, mutton, beef, pork, fish, tofu, egg or minced meat (mixed or not). It is then mixed with coconut, coconut milk, and a rich mixture of vegetables and spices. The food is wrapped around the bamboo stick, sugar canes and lemongrass. After which it is grilled and you can enjoy your satay with or without sauce.
